Philly Cheesesteak

A classic Philly cheesesteak made with thin-sliced beef, caramelized onions, melted cheese, and a toasted hoagie roll.

Recipe details

  • Prep: 10 min
  • Cook: 15 min
  • Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak (thinly sliced)
  • Hoagie rolls
  • Provolone or American cheese
  • Onion (sliced)
  • Green bell pepper (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Slice steak very thin (freeze slightly if needed).
  2. Heat a pan and cook onions (and peppers) until soft.
  3. Add steak, season, and cook until browned.
  4. Add cheese and let it melt over the meat.
  5. Toast hoagie roll and assemble the sandwich.

Tips

  • Freezing the ribeye for 10–15 minutes makes thin slicing easier.
  • Cook onions low and slow for better caramelization.
  • Provolone is classic, but American melts super smoothly.

The Weeknd Experience

This project is an interactive narrative experience exploring The Weeknd’s album eras through mood, visuals, atmosphere, and creative coding. Each page is designed to feel like stepping into a different emotional world rather than just reading album information.

About the Project

The goal of this website is to transform album eras into immersive digital spaces. Instead of presenting music in a basic way, this project uses layout, color, movement, and interaction to reflect the emotional identity of each album.

Every era has its own page design, background image, motion system, and atmosphere so the experience feels distinct from one album to another.

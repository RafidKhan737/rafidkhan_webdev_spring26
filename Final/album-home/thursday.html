Album Experience

Thursday

Thursday feels like a relationship you know is not real, but you keep returning to it anyway — slow, lonely, emotionally distant, and trapped in a cycle of wanting more while accepting less.

you only matter on thursday — and you keep waiting for it

The Core Feeling

Emotional attachment without commitment. Jealousy, mixed signals, quiet heartbreak, and the exhaustion of being somebody’s option instead of their priority.

Sound & Themes

Thursday is slower and more emotionally drained than House of Balloons. Its hazy production, distant vulnerability, and looping atmosphere feel like overthinking in the middle of the night.

Lack of commitment The page reflects being emotionally invested in something unstable and undefined.
Repetition and waiting The looping rain visual mirrors the feeling of returning to the same emotions again and again.
Power imbalance One person always seems to care more, while the other controls the rhythm of the relationship.

Album Experience

Echoes of Silence

Echoes of Silence feels like what is left after everything falls apart — not heartbreak, but the silence after the feeling is gone, where only distance, numbness, and fading traces remain.

the silence stays longer than the feeling
Fragments appear, fade, and return like thoughts you cannot fully hold onto.

The Core Feeling

Total emotional numbness. Isolation after chaos and heartbreak. Accepting emptiness instead of fighting it. This is the aftermath, where the noise is gone and only the echo remains.

Emptiness
Isolation
Emotional detachment
Regret without urgency
Silence after the collapse
Loss of connection The page reflects distance from other people and from yourself, like everything meaningful has already slipped away.
Letting things fade Instead of chasing a fix, the album lives in what remains after emotion has burned itself out.
Ghost-like memory Shapes and traces repeat visually to suggest a past that is still present, but no longer fully real.

Track Atmosphere

These songs feel slower, colder, and more haunted than the earlier projects — like sitting alone in the dark while old feelings fade in and out without ever fully disappearing.
